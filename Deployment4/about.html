About Me

Hello! My name is Damien Koon, and I was born in Biloxi, Mississippi, and raised on the Mississippi Gulf Coast. I graduated from ong Beach Senior High School in 2020, during the COVID-19 lockdown. I had an interested high school graduation to say the least. We were all social distanced and I got toilet paper as an official graduation gift. I then went to the Mississippi Gulf Coast Community College to get a degree in Cyber Security, and spent my first year of college entirely remote. Toward the end of my associate's degree in cyber security, I realized I had a passion for mathematics and physics, decided that after I got my degree, I would change my major to physics. I then earned a seperate associate's degree in physics, and transferred to the Florida Institute of Technology. I am now a junior physics student working towards a double degree in Physics and Astronomy.
